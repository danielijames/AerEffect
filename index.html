Privacy Agreement

“InstagramToCameraRoll” is an app created by an independent developer that was created in order to shorten the steps it takes to get media back from instagram. We use instagram's API provided by Facebook.

What Information We Take?

We do not store any information on you or your account. Instagram handles the login flow. We simply display information instagram gives to us and offer saving to your IOS photos functionality.

We do this because too much media is lost toa destroyed phone or sudden change in circumstances. Instagram is a palce wher emost of our lives is documented. If you put something their then taking it back should be easy.

Final Privacy Statement?

We do not accept your data for storage. Instagram handles your login information. We only display media from instagram and save that media to your phone when requested.
